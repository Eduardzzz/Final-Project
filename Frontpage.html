Light Me Up!




How To Play


Click on the Light Bulb: To begin your quest for knowledge, simply click on the light bulb icon. This action triggers the appearance of a question, prompting you to delve into various topics.


Answer the Question: Read the question carefully and reflect on your knowledge. Once you've formulated your answer, you can either type it directly into the pop-up or simply ponder it in your mind.


Learn and Explore: After submitting your answer, take a moment to learn more about the topic at hand. We provide detailed explanations and additional resources to deepen your understanding and encourage further exploration.


Repeat and Enjoy: The fun doesn't stop with one question! Feel free to click on the light bulb again to reveal a new question. With a diverse array of topics and challenges, there's always something new to discover and learn.
